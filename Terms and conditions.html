Booking & Payment

A non-refundable deposit of 30% is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance must be paid no later than 14 days prior to the event date. Failure to pay on time may result in cancellation of services without refund.

Permits & Approvals

The client is responsible for obtaining all necessary permits, permissions, or licenses required for a fireworks display. AKEvents can assist with the application process upon request, but final responsibility lies with the client. If permits are not secured, AKEvents reserves the right to cancel the display, and the client will be liable for any costs incurred.

Site Requirements

A suitable firing site must be provided, with clear access for vehicles and a safe fallout zone. The site must meet legal safety distances as per local regulations. A site inspection may be required in advance.

Weather & Safety

Displays are weather-dependent. In the case of severe weather (e.g., high winds or lightning), AKEvents reserves the right to postpone or cancel the display for safety reasons. If the event is canceled due to weather, AKEvents will make reasonable efforts to reschedule. Refunds are not guaranteed. On-site safety is managed by trained professionals; no unauthorized persons may enter the firing zone.

Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations must be made in writing. The deposit is non-refundable under any circumstances. If cancellation occurs less than 14 days before the event, the full fee is payable.

Force Majeure

AKEvents shall not be held responsible for delays, non-performance, or cancellations due to circumstances beyond our control (e.g., natural disasters, government restrictions, labor strikes).
